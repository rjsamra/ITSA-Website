  • What are the benefits of joining a committee?

    ITSA has always been a hardcore supporter of teamwork and the betterment of one’s potential. Even if you are not known to any concept but want to learn about it, ITSA is the perfect place. From the presidents to the core members, everyone here will help you amplify your perspective to move forward in the technology as well as the real world.

  • How can I join a committee Event?

    Joining and becoming involved in the events is merely simple starting from filling the source forms made available by the team or one can directly contact our heads or any core member in person.

    Most of the events held by ITSA are FREE of cost, you only need to fill up the requirements in the form supplied.

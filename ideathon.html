Ideathon

Terms and Conditions :

Eligibility : Only students studying at Vidyalankar Institute of Technology are allowed to participate
Participation : A group of up to 3 students are allowed
Problem statement : It will be disclosed at the start of the event
Solution : It will include a prototype , a presentation and a report
Plagiarism : All teams need to come up with an unique idea , taking inspiration from various sources is permitted but core idea should be your own
Weightage : Prototype will carry 70% , Presentation will carry 20% and Report will carry 10% of the total weightage
Check-in time : All the teams have to check in at 1:00pm on Sunday the 6th of September 2020, any delay in check-in may lead to immediate disqualification of the concerned team
Submission : The solution to the given problem statement have to be submitted by 6pm on 6th September 2020 , any delay in submission will not be accepted ; in case of such an unfortunate incident please contact the authority in-charge
Prizes and Awards : All participating teams will be given certificates
Winning Team : The winning team from each domain will be given special certificates and their idea will be taken forward up to completion by ITSA and ITSA will have rights to the project along with the team concerned
Disclaimer : Under no circumstances will ITSA be responsible for any direct or indirect effects occurring during the course of the event

We are looking forward to seeing you on 6th September at 1pm
